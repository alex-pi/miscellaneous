EPA Climate Change Indicators

In 2016 the EPA (Environmental Protection Agency) presented an comprehensive report (found here) on the different indicators for climate change. The agency processed and categorized data from other agencies to create this report. Unfortunately, the agency hasn't produced a similar report as of 2020. In that sense, this page is a shout out to the scientific community to keep up the good work.

This page presents some of those indicators and how they are related. The text content are extracts from the EPA report with almost no paraphrasing from my side. The reader should consider that my intention is to present correlations amongst the indicators and not to drive conclusions on this very complex topic.
